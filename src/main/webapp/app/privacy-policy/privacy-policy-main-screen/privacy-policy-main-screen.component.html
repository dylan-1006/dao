Privacy Policy

1.0 What data do we collect?

Our application collects the following data:
  1. Username
  2. Messages
  3. Files/attachments sent in the message
  4. Time spent on the application

2.0 How do we collect your data?

We collect data directly when you:
  1. Create a new account to access the full features of our application.
  2. Use or view our website via your browser’s cookies.
Additionally, we may receive data indirectly by keeping track of the time spent on the application.

3.0 How will we use your data?

We collect your data to:
  1. Display your username to other users in your session.
  2. Keep track of your messages and attachments sent during the session.
  3. Provide and display analytics for your performance.

4.0 How do we store your data?

All data collected through our website is securely stored on servers located within the United Kingdom, hosted by The University of Birmingham's Cloud Provider. We prioritize the privacy and protection of your data, adhering to the data storage regulations set forth by the United Kingdom and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Our data security measures include encryption, strict access controls, regular audits, and compliance with UK regulations like GDPR. We only retain user data until May 2024 and securely delete it afterward, following industry best practices, such as overwriting data or using cryptographic erasure techniques, to ensure that the data is irrecoverable.

5.0 Marketing

We do not use your data for marketing purposes at this time.

6.0 What are your data protection rights?

Our application would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights.
Every user is entitled to the following:
  1. The right to access – You have the right to request our application for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  2. The right to rectification – You have the right to request that our application correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Our application to complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  3. The right to erasure – You have the right to request that our application erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  4. The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that our application restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  5. The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to our application’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  6. The right to data portability – You have the right to request that our application transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

7.0 What are cookies?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org

8.0 How do we use cookies?

Our application uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our website, including:
  1. Keeping you signed in
  2. Understanding how you use our website

9.0 What types of cookies do we use?

Our application uses the functionalities cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and the location you are in. A mix of first-party and third-party cookies is used.

10.0 How to manage cookies?

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

11.0 Privacy policies of other websites

Our application website contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

12.0 Changes to our privacy policy

Our application keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on 28 February 2024.
